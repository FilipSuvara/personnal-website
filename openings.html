1. chapter openings

Openings are the first stage of the game. they usually last 7-12 moves. After that the midgame starts. Human can not learn all of the openings.That is why chess players have a couple of openings in their tool box. you should choose to play the opening that you like to play (after the opening you get in a position which you like to play)

This is my favorite position after 3 moves. If black plays 3.. Bc5 then i play the aggresive line with 4.c3 if black plays 3.. Nf6 then i play the fried liver opening.
