3. chapter Endgame

Endgames are the hardest stage of the chess game. In endgames every move counts. One of the most important things in an endgame is king activity. In this Endgame both kings are far,that is why here black can sacrifice his bishop on C3, if white takes black pushes the pawn and makes a queen on the next move. Some endgames are theoretical. There are a lot of studies of endgames.
