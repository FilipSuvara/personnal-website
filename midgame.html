2. chapter midgame

There are 2 types of middlegames in chess there is a strategy(you play a long fight manouvering pieces and getting them to the best squares. The point of the strategy is to attack the weak spots in the opponents teritory. Goal of the strategy is to go to the winning endgame) and there is tactical(point of the tactical middlegame is to go for the opponents king) middlegame.

Very popular pattern in a midgame is this: greek gift sacrifice. Bishop sacrifices himself on H7 for a huge attack after the king takes knight jumps on g5 to give a check and queen will come soon to checkmate the opponent.
